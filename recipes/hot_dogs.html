Hot Dogs

Quick and delicious, these classic hot dogs are perfect for any casual meal. Ready in minutes with simple ingredients and satisfying taste.

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 4 beef or pork hot dogs
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions (optional)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, and relish to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat a pan or grill over medium heat.
  2. Cook the hot dogs for 5–7 minutes, turning occasionally until evenly browned.
  3. Warm the buns in the microwave or oven for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Place each hot dog inside a bun.
  5. Add toppings like ketchup, mustard, onions, cheese, or relish.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
