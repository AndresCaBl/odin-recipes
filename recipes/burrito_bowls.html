Burrito Bowls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked white or brown rice
  • 1/2 cup cooked black beans
  • 1 cup cooked and seasoned chicken (grilled or shredded)
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels (cooked or canned)
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes or salsa
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped lettuce
  • 1/4 sliced avocado or guacamole
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt (optional)
  • Hot sauce or lime wedges (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Season and cook chicken; grill or pan-fry, then slice or shred.
  3. Warm the black beans and corn.
  4. In a bowl, layer the rice as the base.
  5. Add chicken, beans, corn, diced tomatoes or salsa, and cheese.
  6. Top with lettuce, avocado or guacamole, and any extras like sour cream or hot sauce.
  7. Serve immediately or store in containers for meal prep.
