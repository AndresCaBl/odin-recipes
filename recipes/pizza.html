Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza base or dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20–25 slices of pepperoni
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional, for brushing the crust)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Place the pizza base on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Arrange the pepperoni slices evenly over the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle oregano if using, and brush the crust with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a minute or two, then slice and serve.
